# ESCALATION PROTOCOL
**Status:** IMMUTABLE v1 · READ-ONLY  
**Audience:** All VAs  
**Purpose:** Prevent silent failure and guesswork

---

## When to Escalate (DO NOT POWER THROUGH)

Escalate immediately if:
- something does not match the SOP
- validation fails and you do not understand why
- a page looks wrong after build
- you are unsure whether an action is allowed
- you feel stuck for more than 10 minutes

Guessing is forbidden.

---

## What to Capture Before Escalating

Always include:
- SOP name
- step number
- exact error message (copy/paste)
- screenshot (terminal or browser)
- time and date

Do NOT include:
- passwords
- API tokens
- secrets

---

## How to Escalate

Email the Owner:

**To:** info@spryvc.com  
**Subject:** ESCALATION — [SOP NAME]

Paste:
- what you were trying to do
- what happened
- what you expected to happen

Then STOP.

---

## After You Escalate

Do not:
- retry randomly
- search Google for fixes
- ask another VA

Wait for instruction.
